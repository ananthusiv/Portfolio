It also allowed me to develop patience and empathy, essential traits for designing user-centric products.

Through my journey with Figma and responsive design projects, I learned to craft intuitive, accessible interfaces that focus on the user's needs and preferences. This hands-on experience allowed me to apply both creative and analytical thinking, similar to my tutoring days but in a digital space.

Why UI/UX design? UI/UX design allows me to improve how people interact with digital products. My background in tutoring equipped me with empathy, while my technical skills enable me to craft solutions that make digital experiences smoother and more engaging. I’m excited to collaborate with designers and developers alike, building interfaces that truly empower users.
