Hello!

I’m Ananthu S

Before I became a UX/UI designer, I spent a few years tutoring science subjects. As a freelance home tutor, I enjoyed working one-on-one with high school and higher secondary students, helping them grasp complex concepts in physics and other sciences. I found great satisfaction in developing personalized lesson plans that catered to each student’s unique learning style. It taught me the value of tailoring experiences for individual needs—something that translates well into design work. I aimed to create a learning environment where students felt comfortable asking questions and exploring difficult concepts. Think hands-on experiments and real-world applications. Seeing students improve their problem-solving skills reinforced my passion for simplifying complexity.

Design entered my scene recently, after a formal course in UX/UI.

I’ve always had a curiosity for technology, which led me to web development and, later, user-centered design. Through my journey with Figma and responsive design projects, I learned to craft intuitive, accessible interfaces that focus on the user's needs and preferences. This hands-on experience allowed me to apply both creative and analytical thinking, similar to my tutoring days but in a digital space.

Why UX/UI design?

UX/UI design allows me to improve how people interact with digital products. My background in tutoring equipped me with empathy, while my technical skills enable me to craft solutions that make digital experiences smoother and more engaging. I’m excited to collaborate with designers and developers alike, building interfaces that truly empower users.

Moving forward, I’m excited to collaborate with other designers and developers to create thoughtful, user-friendly interfaces that engage and empower users.
