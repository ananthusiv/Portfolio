Introduction

"I feel overwhelmed, and music is my escape, but finding the right song is exhausting sometimes." – 27-year-old user

The Product
Soul Music is a wellness-focused music app that enhances users' emotional well-being by curating personalized playlists based on their current mood. The AI-driven system uses mood assessments and emotional inputs to create tailored listening experiences, helping users manage emotions through music.

The Users
Target audience: Adults aged 18-35 who rely on music as a form of emotional self-care. These users often experience high levels of stress and use music to manage their mental and emotional health.

The Business Goal
We aimed to improve user engagement by encouraging users to frequently use the app to check their mood and discover new playlists aligned with their emotional state.

How Can We Help Users Find Music for Emotional Well-being?

Exploring innovative solutions to connect emotions with music.

Using AI for better personalization: Users often struggle to find songs that resonate with their exact mood. Our challenge was to create a tool that simplifies this process by assessing emotional states.

The proposed solution: An AI-driven mood assessment tool that generates personalized playlists based on user inputs, ensuring every user feels heard and understood.

An Unexpected Issue

"I saw the 'Create with Soul AI' button, but I didn’t realize it was for personalized playlists."



Exploring challenges faced by users with the "Create with Soul AI" feature.

What didn’t work? Users noticed the "Create with Soul AI" button, but didn’t immediately connect it with creating mood-based playlists. The text near the button wasn’t enough to convey the full function to some users. Users assumed the feature was more about standard playlist generation, not tailored to their emotions.

What did work? By adding more context around the AI's role in curating personalized playlists, we increased user engagement. Additionally, simplifying the button design and labeling led to more intuitive usage.

Helping users discover mood-based playlists

"It would be great if there was a better way to find playlists for my emotions."

Priority Revisions

Although users didn’t face issues finding the button, the main challenge was clarifying the purpose of the "Create with Soul AI" feature.
It was crucial to convey that the feature was designed to generate mood-based, personalized playlists, not just standard playlists.

Idea 1: Add a brief explanation or tooltip

  • Pro: A tooltip or brief description that appears when users hover or tap the button could clarify that the AI generates playlists based on the user's mood.
  • Con: Excessive text could clutter the interface or overwhelm the user, reducing the overall user experience.

Idea 2: Integrate a mood check-in with the button

  • Pro: A simple mood check-in step before creating the playlist could help users connect their emotional state with the playlist generation process, making the feature more intuitive.
  • Con: Adding an extra step might introduce friction for users who want a quick experience without interruption.

Idea 3: Use visual elements to enhance understanding

  • Pro: Adding emoticons or small icons to represent different moods could make it clearer that the AI-generated playlists are based on mood, improving the overall understanding of the feature.
  • Con: The addition of visuals might affect the simplicity of the button design and require careful consideration to avoid cluttering the UI.

Where do we go from here?

Our Vision

We plan to continue improving the AI system to make music suggestions even more accurate and finely tuned to users' unique emotional states. Additionally, we’re considering a broader set of user feedback loops to further refine and enhance playlists based on emotional shifts throughout the day.

Next Steps

I recommend testing the updated UI with new participants to see if these changes make the "Create with Soul AI" feature clearer.

Metrics to Track

  • How often users click the "Create with Soul AI" button after the revisions?
  • Do users engage more with the personalized playlists once they understand the feature better?
  • How has the change impacted user satisfaction and retention?

What I Learned

The importance of clear communication in UX. While the button was visible, making sure users fully understood the functionality required thoughtful adjustments to text and visual elements.
